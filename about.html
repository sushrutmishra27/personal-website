About me

I'm Sushrut, a developer turned marketer. I started out in engineering, writing Apex, JavaScript, and a bit of React and shipping real products. Turns out I liked talking about the product a lot more than building it, so I started writing about it on the side and never really stopped.

So I jumped. Over the next few years I did almost 30 freelance gigs, took two part-time roles, and ran my own agency, all marketing B2B SaaS to technical folks through blogs, SEO, videos, and social. People always ask how I pulled all that off at once. Honestly, I just worked a lot, way too much, and barely looked up from my laptop for a few years. Worth it, though.

Today I'm a developer marketing manager at Bito and Head of Growth at Devable AI. The whole trick is being fluent in both engineering and marketing: my dev years mean I actually get the product and can explain it to a technical crowd without hand-waving. That combination is my edge.
