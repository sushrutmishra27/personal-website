About me

I left a development job to market to developers. The thread that connects both: I've always cared more about why people use software than how it compiles.

I'm a developer turned marketer. I still think in systems, but I spend my days on content, growth, and story, mostly aimed at other developers.

I started in engineering. After studying computer science at the Noida Institute of Engineering & Technology, I worked as a software developer, writing JavaScript, React, Java, and Python and shipping real products. Along the way I realised I was more drawn to the message around the product than to the code inside it.

So I made the switch. Over the next couple of years I took on 30+ freelance marketing gigs, held two part-time roles, and built my own agency, all focused on marketing B2B SaaS to a technical audience through blogs, SEO, and video tutorials.

Today I'm the first content marketing hire at Bito, where I own content end to end for a codebase-aware AI code review product. Being fluent in both engineering and marketing means I can read the codebase and write the story, and that combination is the whole point.
