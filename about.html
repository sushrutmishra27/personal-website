About me

I'm Sushrut, a developer turned marketer. My dev brain still thinks in systems, but I spend my days on content, growth, and story, mostly aimed at other developers and engineers.

I started in engineering. After studying computer science, I worked as a software developer, writing Apex, JavaScript, and a little React, and shipping real products along the way. Somewhere in there I realised I cared more about the message around the product than the code inside it, so I started picking up freelance technical writing on the side.

Eventually I made the switch. Over the next couple of years I took on almost 30 freelance marketing and writing gigs, held two part-time roles, and built my own agency, all focused on marketing B2B SaaS to a technical audience through blogs, SEO, videos, and social content.

Now you might be wondering how one person does freelancing, an agency, and part-time jobs all at once. Honestly? For three or four years my life was nothing but my laptop. No social life, just me at my desk for 16 to 17 hours a day. I do not recommend the sleep schedule, but I would do it again.

Today I'm the first content marketer at Bito AI, where I own content end to end. Being fluent in both engineering and marketing means my time as a developer helps me actually understand technical products and explain them to a technical audience. That combination is my edge.
