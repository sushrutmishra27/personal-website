Reading list

Books that shaped how I think about marketing, writing, and building. A living list, updated as I go.

Marketing & growth

April Dunford

Obviously Awesome

The clearest book on positioning I've read, and I re-read it often.

Donald Miller

Building a StoryBrand

Why clarity beats cleverness in almost every piece of marketing.

Seth Godin

This Is Marketing

Marketing as service, not noise. Changed how I frame everything.

Robert Cialdini

Influence

The persuasion classic every marketer should read at least once.

Writing & content

Ann Handley

Everybody Writes

A practical bible for anyone who writes for a living.

William Zinsser

On Writing Well

The book that taught me to cut, then cut again.

Chip & Dan Heath

Made to Stick

Why some ideas survive and others die. Useful for every headline.

Mindset & craft

Austin Kleon

Show Your Work!

The nudge that got me building in public in the first place.

James Clear

Atomic Habits

How the small daily reps actually compound. The writing ones especially.

Eric Jorgenson

The Almanack of Naval Ravikant

On leverage, judgment, and playing long games. I keep coming back to it.

Got a book I should read?

I'm always taking recommendations, especially on marketing, code, and craft.
